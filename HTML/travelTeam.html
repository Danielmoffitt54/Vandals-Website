travel team

We offer competitive summer travel teams for high school boys and girls that are rising freshman-rising seniors. For our travel teams we are focused on personal and player growth. If you are interested in playing in college, playing on a competitive summer travel team can take your game to another level.

* All players must have a current US Lacrosse membership. Registration is processed through the Register tab. Payments for memberships are processed through the store.
