lax bash

7v7 boys & girls high school tournament

location:

michael o. buchanon park

bowling green, KY

tournament guidelines

  • 2 Attack, 2 Middies, 2 Defenders, 1 Goalie.
  • Game Format: 20 Minute Running Halves, 5 Minute Halftime.
  • 7v7 Field: 40x60 yards.
  • Food Trucks & Concessions on site.
  • All players must have a current US Lacrosse Membership.
  • High School and Club teams welcome.
  • $550 per team.
  • Deadline to Register and pay is June 1st.
  • Player Waivers required and can be printed from site.
Any questions please contact us
