We offer summer travel teams, and position specific private lessons for boys and girls. Our vision is to grow the game in Southern Kentucky. We travel to surrounding areas showcasing the Vandals and exposing local players to quality play outside of our state.

Benefits of Being a Vandal

  • Be a part of a cohesive team with a great atmosphere.
  • Develop as a person and player.
  • Countless practice hours.
  • Compete with and against some of the best in Kentucky and our surrounding states.
  • Multiple chances to be seen by college coaches and recruiters at tournaments we attend.
  • Knowledgeable staff to help your player navigate through the recruiting process.
